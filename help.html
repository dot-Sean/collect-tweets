Help

  1. Enter in the Control Panel url here
  2. Place a hash tag in the search box an press "search". (Remember, you DON'T have to include the "#" symbol in the box)
  3. Wait a couple of seconds (Remember, is a live search on twitter, it could take more than just a couple of seconds)
  4. When the result are displayed you can edit the status, and check whether you want a tweet to be seen or not in the messague card
  5. Once you are done, hit the SAVE button for storing the information
  6. You can see an example in here
  7. Inside the example take a look of the source code, and seek for the line that contains var hashtag = "GMA"; and replace "GMA" with the hash tag you want it to draw, like this: var hashtag = "TEST-HASHTAG":
  8. REMEMBER: nothing will show if you haven't save anything yet in the contro panel
